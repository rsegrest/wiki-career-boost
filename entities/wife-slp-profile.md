### Pay Context

- **Local market average** (Huntsville/Madison): $42–43/hr (~$87–89K/yr) — Indeed, Vivian
- **She is $7–8/hr below local average** = ~$14,000–16,000/year below market
- **National SNF median** (ASHA 2025): $105,500/yr
- **National all-setting SLP median** (BLS May 2024): $95,410/yr
- She is **$15–35K below national medians** for her credential level

### The CCC Raise That Didn't Happen

- Rick recalls discussion of a ~20% raise upon CCC certification
- Industry standard: $3–7/hr bump (10–20%) upon completing Clinical Fellowship and earning CCC
- Going from $35 → $42/hr would be exactly 20% — and would merely bring her TO market rate
- Restore did not deliver this raise
- This is consistent with Restore's company-wide pattern (see [[restore-therapy-services]])

### Leave Context

- 80 hrs/year is below industry typical (2–3 weeks / 10–15 days starting PTO, often separate from sick)
- Restore reviews mention ~4 hrs PTO per biweekly pay period = ~104 hrs/yr for 1–4 yrs tenure
- She may be below even Restore's own standard, or in a different accrual tier
- Restore forces PTO usage during low census to guarantee 32-hour weeks
- She is frequently "maxed out" on leave — cannot take time off when needed
- Only 4 paid holidays (many healthcare employers offer 6–8+)

## Work Environment

- She is one of the few staying during turnover
